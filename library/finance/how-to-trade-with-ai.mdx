# How to Trade with AI the Right Way

**NexusTrade's practical methodology for AI-assisted trading — with a direct assessment of the mistakes most AI traders make.**

## What it covers

This blog post from NexusTrade lays out an opinionated framework for using AI in trading the right way — covering where AI adds genuine value in the research and analysis pipeline, where it doesn't, and what the common failure patterns look like among traders who misapply it. The original title includes a pointed take specifically about OpenClaw users as a case study in what not to do, making it more direct than the average methodology post.

The article is notable for its practicality: it doesn't advocate for or against AI trading in the abstract, but describes specific, actionable methodology — when to trust model output, when to override it, and how to structure the human-AI collaboration in a trading workflow.

<Tip>
  **Read this when** you're incorporating AI into a trading or research process and want a methodology grounded in real practice — particularly useful as a contrast to theoretical or benchmark-based arguments for AI trading.
</Tip>
