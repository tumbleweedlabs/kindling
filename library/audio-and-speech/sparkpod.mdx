## What it is

SparkPod is an AI podcast creation platform that converts source materials — PDFs, YouTube videos, web articles, and text notes — into fully produced audio episodes. The workflow covers every step: intelligent content extraction, script generation and editing, pacing controls, multi-host conversation formatting, and final audio rendering with premium AI voices. Creator and Studio plans add API access, making it usable in automated content pipelines.

The platform targets content creators, course builders, marketers, and organizations that want to repurpose existing written or video content as audio without manual recording. The free tier allows 5 podcasts per month; paid plans scale from $10/month (100 podcasts) to $25/month (500 podcasts) for higher volume.

<Tip>
  **Use this when** you need to repurpose a backlog of written content — blog posts, documentation, research reports — as audio at volume, especially if API automation is required for the pipeline.
</Tip>
