## What it is

ElevenLabs is the leading AI voice platform, offering text-to-speech synthesis across 70+ languages, voice cloning from short audio samples, speech-to-text via its Scribe model, AI music generation, sound effects, and conversational AI agent infrastructure. Everything is accessible through a web studio for manual use or a developer API for production integration. It has become the default choice for podcast producers, game developers, content creators, and AI application builders who need high-quality speech output.

Pricing ranges from a free tier to an enterprise plan at $990/month, with mid-tier plans at $11/month (Creator) and $99/month (Pro) covering most professional use cases. The voice library includes thousands of pre-built voices across accents and styles, and the cloning feature supports creating branded voices from as little as a minute of audio.

<Tip>
  **Use this when** you need production-quality voice output for content, products, or applications — especially where the voice needs to sound natural in a specific language, accent, or style that generic TTS doesn't handle well.
</Tip>
