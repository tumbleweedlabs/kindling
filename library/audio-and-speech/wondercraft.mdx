## What it is

Wondercraft is an AI production studio that integrates multiple generative AI models — video generation, digital avatars, voice cloning, image synthesis, music, and sound effects — into structured business content workflows. It started as an audio-first platform (podcasts, narrations) and has evolved into a video-first product targeting marketing, HR, L&D, and communications teams. Use cases include training videos, product explainers, onboarding content, and podcast series — all without a video editing background.

An API is available for teams that need to automate content production at scale or embed generation into internal tools. The freemium tier doesn't require a credit card, making it accessible for evaluation before committing to a paid plan.

<Tip>
  **Use this when** your team regularly produces instructional or marketing video content and wants to move production in-house using AI — especially for L&D or onboarding content where quality needs to be consistent but volume is high.
</Tip>
