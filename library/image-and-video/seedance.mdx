## What it is

Seedance 2.0 is ByteDance's state-of-the-art image-to-video generation model, capable of producing high-quality cinematic video clips from a single still image. It offers fine-grained control over camera motion, scene dynamics, and video duration, making it notable for production-quality creative work rather than just demonstration outputs. The model is accessible through Higgsfield AI's platform and through a community-built ComfyUI workflow for local or cloud-based generation.

Seedance sits firmly in the Models layer — it's a foundation model for video generation, not an end-user product in itself. The accessible interfaces (Higgsfield, ComfyUI) represent the Applications and Infrastructure layers that put the model to work.

<Tip>
  **Use this when** you need high-quality image-to-video animation for creative, marketing, or product visualization work and want one of the current frontier models rather than an older baseline.
</Tip>
